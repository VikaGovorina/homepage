About me

Don't let the innocent face fool you. I'm a professional nap-taker, a string-chasing champion, and the primary reason no pen is ever left on the desk for long. My days are spent napping, plotting, and graciously accepting treats from my loyal staff (that's you).

My work

The Treat Dispenser Prototype

Behold my latest invention! This section features "blueprint" drawings and videos of my various attempts to build a better, cat-operated treat dispenser. Spoiler: most designs involve knocking something over until a human gives in.

The Midnight Zoomies Tracker

A fun, animated timeline that visualizes my nightly 3 AM bursts of energy. This project uses paw-print icons and silly graphs to answer the age-old question: "Just how many laps did he do last night?"

The Box Investigation Unit

A scientific catalog of every box I have ever tested. Includes my official "Paw Rating" for sturdiness, snugness, and overall existential fulfillment. No box is too big or too small for my rigorous analysis.

The Noisy Bird Analysis

My ongoing research into the "feathered menace" outside the window. This blog series features my detailed commentary (via photo captions) on bird behavior, squirrel antics, and my flawless plans to catch them all (if the window wasn't in the way).

The Great Blanket Fort

A photo gallery dedicated to my most impressive architectural achievement: the blanket fort. Witness the construction, the cozy interior, and the eventual, graceful collapse. It's a masterpiece of temporary engineering.

The Sunbeam Locator 3000

An interactive map of the house that tracks, in real-time (according to me), the best sunny spots for napping throughout the day. Features a "Paw-ndex" forecast for optimal warmth and comfort.
